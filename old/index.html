Software Developer, Engineer, niche skill collector.

I am a trained software developer with several years of professional experience building full stack web based applications. These applications ranged from operational support software to detailed energy data analytics which included both structured SQL and unstructured data. While software development accounts for the bulk of my professional career, it is far from the only set of skills in my repertoire. Working primarily with startup companies has provided me with multifaceted skillsets, from frontend UI/UX development to bare metal electrical/mechanical engineering work. While earning my Master's degree at University of Massachusetts Lowell, I developed a passion for 3D printing and its adjacent technologies and wrote one of the first cloud-based slicing engines, consisting of software that turns computer models into 3D printer compatible code. At Zea Biosciences I built a platform which took both user and sensor data to do live plant condition analysis. This enabled technicians to make custom tailored solutions specific to each plant. Outside of work, I am an avid cyclist and can regularly be found out on a group ride or on Zwift.
